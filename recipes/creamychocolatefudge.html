Creamy Chocolate Fudge

Description

Creamy chocolate fudge is the ultimate indulgence, offering a rich, velvety texture that melts in your mouth. Made by carefully blending chocolate, butter, and condensed milk, this decadent treat balances sweetness with deep cocoa flavor. The key to achieving its signature smoothness lies in the precise cooking and cooling process, ensuring the perfect consistency without graininess. Whether enjoyed on its own or incorporated into desserts, chocolate fudge is a timeless favorite that delivers comfort with every bite.

Beyond its irresistible taste, creamy chocolate fudge carries a nostalgic charm, often associated with homemade treats and holiday traditions. From classic recipes passed down through generations to modern variations featuring nuts, caramel, or sea salt, fudge remains a versatile delight that caters to every preference. Whether gifted in beautifully wrapped boxes or savored as a personal indulgence, its rich, chocolatey goodness continues to bring joy and satisfaction to chocolate lovers everywhere.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Whole milk or Evaporated milk
  • Butter
  • Vanilla extract
  • Unsweetened or semi-sweet chocolate
  • Milk choclate chips
  • Marshmallows or marshmallow creme

Steps

  1. Prepare the pan – Line an 8- or 9-inch square baking pan with foil or parchment paper for easy removal.
  2. Melt ingredients – In a saucepan over low heat, combine chocolate chips and sweetened condensed milk, stirring until smooth.
  3. Add flavor – Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract and any optional ingredients like nuts.
  4. Pour and spread – Transfer the mixture into the prepared pan, spreading it evenly.
  5. Chill – Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or until firm.
  6. Cut and serve – Lift the fudge from the pan, remove the foil, and cut into squares.
